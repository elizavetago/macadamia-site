Services

Pump stations

Control cabinets for booster pumps are designed to automatically maintain the required pressure in the discharge pipeline. Control is based on a pressure sensor with pump speed regulation. Dry-run protection is provided in all operating modes.

Circulation pumps

Control cabinets for circulation pumps are used to maintain a defined differential pressure in the system. Control is based on two pressure sensors or a differential pressure switch, while the pump starting method may vary. Dry-run protection is included.

Drainage pumps

Control cabinets for drainage pumps are intended for automatic control of submersible pumps used for drainage and wastewater removal. A typical configuration includes two pumps controlled by four float level switches, with direct or soft start options. The cabinet provides dry-run protection and pump alternation.

Well pumps

Control cabinets for well pumps are designed to control submersible pumps and maintain stable water pressure. Control is based on variable frequency regulation, with individual dry-run protection for each well using level relays. Typically, one control cabinet is intended for one well.

Irrigation pumps

Control cabinets for irrigation pumps are used in automatic irrigation systems. Pump operation is started by an external control signal, while performance is typically regulated using a variable frequency drive. The cabinet ensures dry-run protection and stable system operation.
